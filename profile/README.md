We're building the infrastructure for business memories.

## The problem

AI today is smart, but forgetful. Like Leonard Shelby from Memento. Lethal but temporally broken. 

We're fixing that.

## Why this matters

The best AI research labs have PhDs writing papers about what's theoretically possible. We're more interested in what's practically deployable. There's a gap between a model that wins benchmarks and a system that transforms how a company operates. We live in that gap.

## Who we're looking for

We don't care where you went to school. We care whether you can ship.

If you've ever looked at how the big labs do things and thought "I could do this better"—prove it. We're building something that lets scrappy teams outmaneuver trillion-dollar incumbents.

The work is hard. The problems are real. The impact is immediate.
